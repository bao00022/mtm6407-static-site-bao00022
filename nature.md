# Nature

## Still on Fire

**Location:** Rideau River Trail, Ottawa

A single red leaf rests among the green, lit from behind by afternoon sun. The backlight reveals every vein, turning something ordinary into something luminous. Autumn does not fade quietly.

## The Other Half

**Location:** Rideau River Trail, Ottawa

Bare branches reach into still water, and the reflection below completes them. It is hard to tell where the tree ends and the river begins. The surface holds everything the sky does not.

![The Other Half](/images/nature-02.jpg)
